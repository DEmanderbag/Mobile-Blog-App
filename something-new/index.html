The following is the original piece I wrote for CNN last year entrepreneurs, also known as "digital nomads." Since the whole
"Work Online, Travel the World and Live the Dream" angle had been done to death in a million places, I decided to
go the other direction and show the
dark side of being a digital nomad -
the challenges one faces, the emotional pitfalls, the social sacrifices.

Predictably, CNN hated it. The editors hacked it to pieces and asked me to rewrite sections to make it less gloomy.
But I was always a font of the original, so I'm posting it here unabridged.
